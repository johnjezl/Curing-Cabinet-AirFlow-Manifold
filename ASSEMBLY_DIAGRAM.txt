ASSEMBLY SEQUENCE:

    1. Print all parts
    2. (If split base) Assemble 9 sections with bolts + gasket
    3. Drill holes in freezer top (9× 38mm holes)
    4. Install intake tubes from below freezer
    5. Place base on freezer top
    6. Snap transition onto base
    7. Snap sensor chamber onto transition
    8. Mount sensor PCB in chamber
    9. Snap fan adapter onto chamber
   10. Mount 120mm fan on adapter
   11. Connect power and sensor
   12. Test and calibrate

    Overall Assembly:
    - Width: 510mm (base) → 130mm (fan adapter)
    - Depth: 510mm (base) → 130mm (fan adapter)
    - Height: ~290mm (base to fan)

    Individual Components:
    - Base: 510×510×43mm (OR 9× 170×170×43mm)
    - Transition: 510×510×150mm (tapered to 48×48mm)
    - Sensor chamber: 48×48×80mm
    - Fan adapter: 48×48×44mm (expanding to 130×130mm)
    - Intake tubes: Ø38mm × 60mm (9 pieces)

    Key Measurements:
    - Tube spacing: 137.5mm centers
    - Tube ID: 35mm (intake area: 962mm² each)
    - Sensor chamber: 42×42mm (1764mm²)
    - Speed multiplier: 4.91×
    - Total intake area: 8659mm²
